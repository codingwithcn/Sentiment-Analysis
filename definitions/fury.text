unrestrained or violent anger, rage, passion, or the like: The gods unleashed their fury on the offending mortal.violence; vehemence; fierceness: the fury of a hurricane; a fury of creative energy.Furies, Classical Mythology. minor female divinities: the daughters of Gaia who punished crimes at the instigation of the victims: known to the Greeks as the Erinyes or Eumenides and to the Romans as the Furiae or Dirae.
